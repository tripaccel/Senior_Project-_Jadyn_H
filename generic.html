Equipment

Recently, I was working on a film with some friends from school.This project is actually my second project since transfering back and I was excited to do it. This the equipment we used for the film.

These pieces are additions to the saramonic mics. There are two mufflers for the mics, there is an jack for phone as well as a jack for camera.

This black tripod has definetly seen better days. It has a detachedable phone holder. Every section of it is able to be adjusted, whether its the height of where the camera is located.

On the bottom, this connector resembles a lighting cable for iphones. Plug into iphones that are 14 and under. Doing so, when you are in your camera with bluetooth on, the iphone will get the audio from the mics, as well as from the iphone. The top connector resembles a USB-C, connecting this to iphone 15 or 16 as well as android devices. Make sure bluetooth is on when recording a video.

When you open the saramonic box, you will find two attachment mics and a receiver. The receiver has a visual interface, letting you know the batteries of the mic, if the mic is picking up and the volume of the mics.

After opening up the packaging, you will see this box. This is the saramonic blink500B2, comes with a wireless lavalier, microphone for iphone, android, type-C smartphone camera, 492ft range, 20h battery life clip on wireless lapel mic for live streaming video. You can find this exact equipment on Amazon or you can find related equipment at a Best Buy near you.
